# Invasion Protocol - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Epic orchestral, dark fantasy
- **Tempo**: Medium, dramatic
- **Mood**: Intense, strategic
- **Style**: Medieval battle music

## Sound Effects List

### Unit Actions
- **Unit Spawn**: Summoning sound
- **Unit Movement**: Footsteps (varies by unit)
- **Unit Attack**: Weapon sounds
- **Unit Death**: Death cry

### Tower Actions
- **Tower Attack**: Arrow/magic projectile
- **Tower Destroyed**: Explosion

### Game Events
- **Currency Gain**: Coin sound
- **Level Complete**: Victory fanfare
- **Level Failed**: Defeat horn

### UI
- **Unit Select**: Click
- **Button Click**: Stone tap
- **Insufficient Funds**: Negative buzz

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Unit spawn sounds instant
- **Spatial**: Sounds positioned based on location
- **Layered**: Multiple units create battle ambience

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
