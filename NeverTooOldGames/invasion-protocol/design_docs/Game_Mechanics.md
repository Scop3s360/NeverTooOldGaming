# Invasion Protocol - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Select Unit**: Choose from available unit types
2. **Spawn Unit**: Click to send unit on path
3. **Manage Currency**: Balance spending and saving
4. **Strategic Timing**: Send units in waves or individually

## Rules and Constraints
- Currency generates passively over time
- Each unit costs currency to spawn
- Units auto-move along path
- Enemy towers attack units automatically
- Units that reach throne win the level
- Units that die don't refund currency

## Win Conditions
- X units reach the throne (varies by level)
- Destroy all towers (alternative win)

## Lose Conditions
- Run out of currency with no units alive
- Time limit expires (optional)

## Scoring System
- **Units Survived**: Bonus points
- **Time Bonus**: Faster completion = more points
- **Currency Remaining**: Efficiency bonus

## Star Rating
- **3 Stars**: Complete quickly with currency remaining
- **2 Stars**: Complete with moderate efficiency
- **1 Star**: Complete level

## Unit Types & Stats

### Grunt
- Cost: 10
- HP: 50
- Speed: Medium
- Damage: Low

### Tank
- Cost: 50
- HP: 200
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: Medium

### Runner
- Cost: 15
- HP: 30
- Speed: Fast
- Damage: Low

### Healer
- Cost: 30
- HP: 40
- Speed: Medium
- Ability: Heals nearby units

### Siege
- Cost: 75
- HP: 100
- Speed: Slow
- Damage: Very High

## Difficulty Progression
- Early levels: Simple paths, few towers
- Mid levels: Complex paths, more towers
- Late levels: Strategic unit combinations required
