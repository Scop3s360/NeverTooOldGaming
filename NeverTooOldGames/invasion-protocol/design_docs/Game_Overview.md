# Invasion Protocol - Game Overview

## Title
**Invasion Protocol**

## Tagline
"Command the Horde - Breach the Fortress"

## High-Level Concept
Reverse tower defense where you play as the invading army. Send waves of units to overwhelm castle defenses and reach the throne. Strategic unit deployment replaces tower placement, flipping the genre on its head.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Tower defense fans
- Strategy game enthusiasts
- Players who enjoy role reversal

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (touch-friendly strategy)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Level**: 5-10 minutes
- **10 Levels**: 60-90 minutes
- **Replayability**: High (different strategies)

## Monetization
- **Model**: Paid ($2.99-3.99) or Free with donation
- **No IAPs**: Never
- **No Ads**: Clean experience
