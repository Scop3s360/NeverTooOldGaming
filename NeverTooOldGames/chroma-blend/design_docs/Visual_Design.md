# Chroma Blend - Visual Design

## Art Style
- Vibrant, educational
- Paint splatter effects
- Artistic, colorful aesthetic
- Clear color differentiation

## Color Palette
- **Primary Colors**: Bright red, blue, yellow
- **Secondary Colors**: Purple, orange, green
- **Background**: Soft white or cream
- **UI**: Complementary colors

## UI/UX Layout

### Game Screen
- Grid (center)
- Move counter (top)
- Objective display (top)
- Star rating preview
- Undo button

### Level Select
- Grid of 10 levels
- Star ratings displayed
- Locked/unlocked indicators
- Color-coded difficulty

## Animation Requirements
- **Color Merge**: Swirl animation, colors blend
- **Match Clear**: Tiles burst into paint splatter
- **Tiles Fall**: Smooth gravity animation
- **Combo**: Chain reaction effects
- **Level Complete**: Celebration with paint explosion

## Visual Feedback
- **Valid Merge**: Colors swirl together
- **Invalid Merge**: Shake, red X
- **Match Detected**: Tiles glow
- **Combo**: Screen effects intensify
