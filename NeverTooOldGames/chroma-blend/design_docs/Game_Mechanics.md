# Chroma Blend - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Drag to Merge**: Drag one color onto adjacent color
2. **Create Secondary Colors**: Mix primaries
3. **Match 3+**: Align same colors
4. **Undo**: Revert last move (limited)

## Color Mixing Rules
- **Red + Blue = Purple**
- **Red + Yellow = Orange**
- **Blue + Yellow = Green**
- **Cannot mix secondary colors**
- **Cannot mix same colors**

## Rules and Constraints
- Can only merge adjacent tiles
- Must create valid secondary color
- Match 3+ to clear
- Limited moves per level
- Gravity pulls tiles down

## Win Conditions
- Complete level objective within move limit
- Objectives vary: clear X tiles, create Y secondary colors, etc.

## Lose Conditions
- Run out of moves before completing objective
- Can retry unlimited times

## Scoring System
- **Merge**: 10 points
- **Match 3**: 30 points
- **Match 4**: 60 points
- **Match 5+**: 100+ points
- **Combo**: Multiplier for chain reactions

## Star Rating
- **3 Stars**: Complete with moves to spare
- **2 Stars**: Complete near move limit
- **1 Star**: Complete at move limit

## Difficulty Progression (10 Levels)
- **Level 1-3**: Simple objectives, generous moves
- **Level 4-6**: Complex objectives, moderate moves
- **Level 7-9**: Challenging objectives, limited moves
- **Level 10**: Master challenge
