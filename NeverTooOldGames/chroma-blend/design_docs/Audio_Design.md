# Chroma Blend - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Upbeat, playful
- **Tempo**: Medium, cheerful
- **Mood**: Creative, artistic
- **Style**: Light, whimsical

## Sound Effects List

### Color Mixing
- **Merge**: Satisfying "blend" sound
- **Create Secondary**: Special chime per color
- **Invalid Merge**: Gentle negative tone

### Matching
- **Match 3**: Pleasant pop
- **Match 4**: Bigger pop
- **Match 5+**: Explosive pop
- **Combo**: Ascending chimes

### Game Events
- **Level Complete**: Victory jingle
- **Star Earned**: Twinkle sound
- **Level Failed**: Sympathetic sound

### UI
- **Button Click**: Soft tap
- **Undo**: Reverse whoosh

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Merge sounds instant
- **Layered**: Combos create musical sequences
- **Satisfying**: Clear audio rewards

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
