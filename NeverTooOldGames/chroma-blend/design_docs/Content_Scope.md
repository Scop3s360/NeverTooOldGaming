# Chroma Blend - Content Scope

## Levels (10 Total)

### Level 1: Tutorial
- Objective: Create 5 secondary colors
- Moves: 15
- Introduces color mixing

### Level 2: Purple Focus
- Objective: Clear 10 purple tiles
- Moves: 12

### Level 3: Orange Focus
- Objective: Clear 10 orange tiles
- Moves: 12

### Level 4: Green Focus
- Objective: Clear 10 green tiles
- Moves: 12

### Level 5: Mixed Colors
- Objective: Create 3 of each secondary color
- Moves: 18

### Level 6: Chain Reaction
- Objective: Create 5 combos
- Moves: 15

### Level 7: Color Master
- Objective: Clear 30 tiles total
- Moves: 20

### Level 8: Limited Moves
- Objective: Clear 20 tiles
- Moves: 10 (challenging)

### Level 9: Rainbow
- Objective: Have all 6 colors on board simultaneously
- Moves: 15

### Level 10: Master Challenge
- Objective: Score 1000 points
- Moves: 25
