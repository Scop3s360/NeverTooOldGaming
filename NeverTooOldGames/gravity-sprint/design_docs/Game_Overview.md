# Gravity Sprint - Game Overview

## Title
**Gravity Sprint**

## Tagline
"Flip Your World - Defy Gravity, Master the Sprint"

## High-Level Concept
An endless runner where gravity is your tool, not jumping. Players flip between floor and ceiling to navigate through increasingly difficult obstacle patterns. The game combines simple one-button controls with brain-bending spatial awareness challenges.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Endless runner fans
- Players who enjoy reflex-based challenges
- Fans of minimalist arcade games

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile port (perfect for touch controls)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Session**: 5-10 minutes per run
- **Total Content**: Endless replayability
- **Challenge Mode**: 10 levels (5-15 minutes)

## Monetization
- **Model**: Paid ($1.99-2.99) or Free with optional donation
- **No IAPs**: Never
- **No Ads**: Clean experience
