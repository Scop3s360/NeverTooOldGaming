# Gravity Sprint - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Electronic, synthwave
- **Tempo**: Fast-paced, energetic
- **Mood**: Intense, focused
- **Dynamic**: Music intensity increases with speed

## Sound Effects List

### Player Actions
- **Gravity Flip**: Whoosh/swoosh sound
- **Landing**: Soft thud when switching surfaces
- **Movement**: Subtle running/sliding sound

### Game Events
- **Death**: Crash/explosion sound
- **Near Miss**: Tension sound (close call)
- **Speed Increase**: Ascending pitch sound
- **Checkpoint**: Milestone sound

### Challenge Mode
- **Level Start**: Ready/go sound
- **Level Complete**: Victory fanfare
- **Level Unlock**: Unlock chime

### UI
- **Button Click**: Soft beep
- **Menu Navigate**: Subtle tick
- **Restart**: Quick whoosh

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Gravity flip sound plays instantly
- **Spatial**: Obstacle sounds based on proximity
- **Layered**: Multiple sounds for complex situations

## Ambient Sounds
- Subtle sci-fi ambience
- Wind/rushing air sound
- Electronic hum

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
