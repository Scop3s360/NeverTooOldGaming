# Gravity Sprint - Monetization

## Business Model
**Paid Game** ($1.99-2.99) or **Free with Optional Donation**

## Pricing Strategy
- **itch.io**: Pay what you want (suggested $1.99)
- **Steam**: $2.99 fixed price
- **Mobile**: $0.99 one-time purchase

## What Players Get
- Complete game
- Endless mode
- 10 challenge levels
- All features unlocked
- No ads
- No IAPs

## Monetization Rules (NEVER)
- ❌ No in-app purchases
- ❌ No ads
- ❌ No premium currency
- ❌ No pay-to-unlock levels
- ❌ No subscriptions

## Why Paid?
- Premium experience
- No compromises on gameplay
- Fair value for content
- Supports development

## Success Metrics
- Download/purchase count
- Player retention
- Positive reviews
- Completion rate
