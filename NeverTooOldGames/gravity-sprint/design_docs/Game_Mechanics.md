# Gravity Sprint - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Gravity Flip**: Press Space/Click to flip between floor and ceiling
2. **Restart**: Quick restart after death
3. **Pause**: ESC to pause

## Rules and Constraints
- Character moves forward automatically
- Speed increases gradually over time
- Cannot control horizontal movement
- Can only flip gravity (no jumping)
- Instant death on collision with obstacles

## Win Conditions
- **Endless Mode**: No win condition, survive as long as possible
- **Challenge Mode**: Reach the end of each level

## Lose Conditions
- Collide with any obstacle
- Fall off screen (if applicable)

## Scoring System
- **Distance**: 1 point per unit traveled
- **Survival Time**: Bonus points for time survived
- **Speed Multiplier**: Higher speeds = more points
- **Challenge Completion**: Bonus points for completing levels

## Difficulty Progression

### Endless Mode
- Speed increases every 10 seconds
- Obstacle density increases
- More complex patterns appear
- Maximum speed cap at 2x starting speed

### Challenge Mode (10 Levels)
- Level 1-3: Tutorial patterns, slow speed
- Level 4-6: Medium complexity, medium speed
- Level 7-9: Complex patterns, fast speed
- Level 10: Boss level - extreme challenge

## Obstacle Types
- **Static Blocks**: Fixed position obstacles
- **Spikes**: Ceiling/floor hazards
- **Moving Platforms**: Obstacles that move
- **Gaps**: Require precise timing
- **Combo Obstacles**: Require multiple flips in sequence
