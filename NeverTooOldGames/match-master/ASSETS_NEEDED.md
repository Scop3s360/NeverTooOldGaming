# Match Master - Asset Requirements

## Overview
This document lists all assets needed for the complete game. Each theme requires cards, background, music, and animations.

---

## Card Specifications
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Size**: 128x128 pixels (or 256x256 for higher quality)
- **Quantity per theme**: 8 unique card designs (for 8 pairs)
- **Style**: Should match the theme aesthetic
- **Card back**: 1 design per theme (what players see when card is face-down)

---

## Theme 0: Retro Pixel Art
**Style**: Bright primary colors, 8-bit game aesthetic, chunky pixels

### Cards (8 unique designs + 1 back):
1. Pixel Heart (red)
2. Pixel Star (yellow)
3. Pixel Mushroom (red/white)
4. Pixel Coin (gold)
5. Pixel Sword (silver/blue)
6. Pixel Shield (blue/silver)
7. Pixel Potion (purple/pink)
8. Pixel Key (gold)
9. Card Back: Retro game controller or "?" block

### Background:
- **File**: `background.png` (720x1280 or scalable)
- **Style**: Dark blue/purple with pixel grid pattern, arcade cabinet aesthetic
- **Elements**: Scanlines, pixel stars, retro game border

### Music:
- **File**: `theme.ogg` or `theme.mp3`
- **Style**: Chiptune/8-bit music
- **Length**: 1-2 minutes (loopable)
- **Mood**: Upbeat, nostalgic, arcade-style

### Animations:
- **File**: Sprite sheet or individual frames
- **Style**: Pixel explosion or "power-up" effect when cards match
- **Duration**: 0.5-1 second

## Where to Get Assets

### Free Resources:
- **OpenGameArt.org** - Free game assets
- **Kenney.nl** - Free game assets (great for prototypes)
- **Freesound.org** - Free sound effects
- **Incompetech.com** - Free music (Kevin MacLeod)
- **itch.io** - Many free/paid asset packs

### AI Generation:
- **DALL-E / Midjourney** - Generate card designs
- **Stable Diffusion** - Free AI image generation
- **Suno / Udio** - AI music generation

### Commission Artists:
- **Fiverr** - Affordable freelancers
- **itch.io** - Game artists
- **ArtStation** - Professional artists

### DIY Tools:
- **Aseprite** - Pixel art editor ($20)
- **Piskel** - Free online pixel art tool
- **GIMP** - Free image editor
- **Audacity** - Free audio editor

## Budget Estimate (if commissioning)

### Per Theme:
- 8 card designs + 1 back: $50-150
- Background: $20-50
- Music track: $30-100
- Animation: $20-50

### Total per theme: ~$120-350
### All 15 themes: ~$1,800-5,250

### Sound effects pack: $20-50
### UI elements: $50-150

**Note**: Prices vary greatly. You can reduce costs by:
- Using free assets
- Creating simple designs yourself
- Using AI generation
- Mixing free and paid assets
