# Match Master - Monetization

## Business Model
**Free-to-Play on itch.io**

## Pricing Strategy
- **Initial Release**: Free download
- **Optional Donation**: "Pay what you want" model on itch.io
- **Suggested Price**: $0 (with optional $1-5 donation)

## Revenue Streams
- **Donations**: Players can choose to support development
- **Future Bundle**: May be included in paid game bundles on Steam
- **Brand Building**: Part of NeverTooOldGames portfolio

## What Players Get for Free
- Complete game with all features
- All 15 themes (unlockable through gameplay)
- All 3 difficulty modes
- Full progression system
- No time limits or restrictions
- No ads

## Monetization Rules (NEVER)
- ❌ No in-app purchases
- ❌ No premium currency
- ❌ No pay-to-unlock themes
- ❌ No advertisements
- ❌ No loot boxes
- ❌ No subscription model
- ❌ No pay-to-win mechanics

## Why Free?
1. **Brand Building**: Establish NeverTooOldGames as player-friendly
2. **Portfolio Piece**: Showcase game development skills
3. **Community Growth**: Build audience for future releases
4. **Learning Project**: Focus on game design, not monetization
5. **Accessibility**: Everyone can enjoy the game

## Future Monetization (If Applicable)
- **Steam Release**: May charge $2-5 for "deluxe" version with extras
- **Mobile Port**: May charge $0.99-2.99 one-time purchase
- **Game Bundles**: Include in multi-game packs

## Success Metrics (Non-Monetary)
- Download count
- Player retention
- Positive reviews/feedback
- Community engagement
- Portfolio value
