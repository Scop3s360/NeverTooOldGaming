# Match Master - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood

### Main Menu
- Upbeat, welcoming background music
- Light, casual tone
- Loopable track (2-3 minutes)

### Gameplay
- Theme-specific background music (15 unique tracks)
- Each theme has matching audio aesthetic
- Calm, non-distracting
- Supports concentration

### Shop
- Cheerful, browsing-friendly music
- Slightly more energetic than gameplay
- Encourages exploration

## Sound Effects List

### Card Interactions
- **Card Flip**: Soft "whoosh" sound
- **Card Match**: Pleasant "ding" or chime
- **Card Mismatch**: Gentle "thud" or negative tone
- **All Pairs Matched**: Victory fanfare

### UI Interactions
- **Button Click**: Soft click
- **Button Hover**: Subtle highlight sound
- **Menu Open**: Swoosh in
- **Menu Close**: Swoosh out

### Shop Interactions
- **Theme Preview**: Preview sound
- **Purchase Success**: Cash register "cha-ching" or unlock sound
- **Insufficient Funds**: Negative buzz

### Game Events
- **Victory**: Celebration music (5-10 seconds)
- **Game Over**: Sympathetic "try again" sound
- **New High Score**: Special achievement sound

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Card flip sound plays instantly on click
- **Delayed**: Match/mismatch sound plays after brief pause
- **Layered**: Multiple sounds can play simultaneously (flip + match)

## Ambient Sounds
- Optional subtle background ambience per theme
- Examples:
  - Ocean theme: Gentle waves
  - Space theme: Cosmic hum
  - Nature theme: Birds chirping
  - Casino theme: Distant casino sounds

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
