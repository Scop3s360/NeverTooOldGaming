# Rhythm Rush - Monetization

## Business Model
**Free on itch.io**

## Pricing Strategy
- **Initial Release**: Free download
- **Optional Donation**: Pay what you want
- **Suggested Price**: $0 (with optional $1-3 donation)

## What Players Get for Free
- Complete game
- All generator types
- Prestige system
- All music tracks
- No time gates
- No ads

## Monetization Rules (NEVER)
- ❌ No in-app purchases
- ❌ No premium currency
- ❌ No pay-to-progress faster
- ❌ No advertisements
- ❌ No energy systems
- ❌ No subscriptions

## Why Free?
- Portfolio building
- Accessible to all players
- Focus on pure gameplay
- Community growth
- No compromises on design

## Success Metrics
- Download count
- Average session length
- Prestige completion rate
- Positive reviews
- Community engagement
