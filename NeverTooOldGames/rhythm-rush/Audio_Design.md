# Rhythm Rush - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Electronic, synthwave, chiptune
- **Tempo**: 120-140 BPM (clear, consistent beat)
- **Mood**: Energetic, upbeat, motivating
- **Tracks**: 3-5 different background tracks

## Sound Effects List

### Click Feedback
- **Perfect On-Beat**: Satisfying "ding" or synth hit
- **Good Timing**: Softer chime
- **Off-Beat**: Dull "thud" or miss sound
- **Combo Milestone**: Special sound at 5x, 10x, etc.

### Generator Sounds
- **Purchase**: Cash register or unlock sound
- **Upgrade**: Power-up sound
- **Production Tick**: Subtle coin sound (can be disabled)

### Prestige
- **Prestige Activate**: Epic ascending sound
- **Prestige Complete**: Victory fanfare

### UI
- **Button Click**: Soft beep
- **Menu Navigate**: Tick sound
- **Tab Switch**: Swoosh

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Click sounds play instantly
- **Layered**: Multiple generators can produce sound simultaneously
- **Spatial**: Beat indicator has distinct sound
- **Dynamic**: Music intensity can increase with combo

## Beat Indicator
- **Metronome Click**: Optional audible beat marker
- **Visual + Audio**: Synchronized beat feedback
- **Adjustable**: Can be turned off for experienced players

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Beat indicator volume (separate)
- Mute toggle
