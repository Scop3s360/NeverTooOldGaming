# Rhythm Rush

## Game Type
Rhythm Idle Clicker

## Theme
Music visualizer aesthetic - pulsing backgrounds, beat-reactive UI, synthwave colors

## Concept
Idle clicker that rewards clicking to a beat. Click on rhythm for bonus multipliers, spam-clicking gives reduced rewards. Rush to build your empire through rhythmic precision.

## Unique Twist
Most idle clickers reward mindless spam-clicking. Rhythm Rush adds skill and timing by rewarding players who click to the beat, making it more engaging and rhythm-game-like while maintaining idle progression.

## Core Mechanics
- Click to earn currency
- Visual/audio beat indicators
- Bonus multiplier for on-beat clicks
- Penalty for off-beat clicks
- Upgrades for passive income
- Different rhythm patterns

## Planned Features
- Beat detection system
- Click timing feedback
- Upgrade shop
- Multiple rhythm tracks
- Combo system
- Prestige mechanics
