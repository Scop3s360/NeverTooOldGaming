# Rhythm Rush - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Electronic, EDM, synthwave
- **Tempo**: 120-140 BPM (clear, clickable beats)
- **Mood**: Energetic, upbeat, motivating
- **Tracks**: 5-8 different rhythm patterns

## Sound Effects List

### Click Feedback
- **Perfect Click**: Satisfying "ding" or synth hit
- **Good Click**: Softer chime
- **Miss Click**: Dull thud or negative tone
- **Combo Milestone**: Special sound at 10x, 25x, 50x

### Generator Sounds
- **Purchase**: Cash register or unlock sound
- **Upgrade**: Power-up sound
- **Generator Active**: Subtle ambient loop per type

### UI Sounds
- **Button Click**: Soft beep
- **Tab Switch**: Swoosh
- **Prestige**: Epic fanfare

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Click sound plays instantly
- **Layered**: Multiple generators create ambient soundscape
- **Dynamic**: Music intensity increases with progression

## Rhythm Tracks
1. **Starter Beat** - Simple 4/4 time
2. **Synth Wave** - Retro 80s vibes
3. **Digital Pulse** - Fast electronic
4. **Chill Groove** - Relaxed tempo
5. **Epic Drop** - High energy
