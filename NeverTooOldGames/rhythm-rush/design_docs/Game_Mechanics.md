# Rhythm Rush - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Click**: Tap/click to earn currency
2. **Purchase Generators**: Buy automated income sources
3. **Upgrade Generators**: Increase generator output
4. **Change Track**: Switch rhythm patterns
5. **Prestige**: Reset for permanent bonuses

## Rules and Constraints
- On-beat clicks earn 2x-5x currency
- Off-beat clicks earn 0.5x currency
- Combo multiplier builds with consecutive on-beat clicks
- Miss a beat = combo resets
- Generators produce currency automatically
- Can click while generators run

## Win Conditions
- No traditional win condition
- Reach endgame milestones
- Max out all generators
- Achieve prestige goals

## Lose Conditions
- No lose condition (idle game)

## Scoring System
- **Base Click**: 1 currency
- **On-Beat Bonus**: 2x-5x multiplier
- **Combo Multiplier**: Up to 10x for long combos
- **Generator Income**: Passive currency per second

## Difficulty Progression
- Early: Manual clicking dominant
- Mid: Balance of clicking and generators
- Late: Generators dominant, clicking for bonuses
- Endgame: Prestige and optimization

## Generator Types
1. **Beat Maker** - 10 currency, 0.1/sec
2. **Rhythm Machine** - 100 currency, 1/sec
3. **Tempo Tower** - 1,000 currency, 10/sec
4. **Melody Factory** - 10,000 currency, 100/sec
5. **Symphony Station** - 100,000 currency, 1,000/sec
6. **Harmony Hub** - 1M currency, 10,000/sec
