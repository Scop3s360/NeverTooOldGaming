# Rhythm Rush - Monetization

## Business Model
**Free on itch.io**

## Pricing Strategy
- Free download
- Optional "pay what you want"
- Suggested donation: $0-2

## What Players Get
- Complete game
- All generators
- All rhythm tracks
- Prestige system
- No ads
- No IAPs

## Monetization Rules (NEVER)
- ❌ No in-app purchases
- ❌ No premium currency
- ❌ No pay-to-progress faster
- ❌ No ads
- ❌ No subscriptions

## Why Free?
- Portfolio piece
- Brand building for NeverTooOldGames
- Accessible to all players
- Focus on gameplay, not monetization

## Success Metrics
- Download count
- Player retention
- Average session length
- Positive reviews
