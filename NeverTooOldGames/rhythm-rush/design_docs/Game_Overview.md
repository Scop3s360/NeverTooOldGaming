# Rhythm Rush - Game Overview

## Title
**Rhythm Rush**

## Tagline
"Click to the Beat - Build Your Empire Through Rhythm"

## High-Level Concept
An idle clicker that rewards rhythmic precision over mindless spam-clicking. Players click to the beat of background music to earn currency and bonuses, then purchase generators that produce passive income. The game combines rhythm game mechanics with idle progression.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Rhythm game fans
- Idle/clicker game enthusiasts
- Music lovers

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (perfect for touch rhythm gameplay)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Session**: 10-30 minutes
- **Total Content**: 5-10 hours to reach endgame
- **Idle Progression**: Earn while away

## Monetization
- **Model**: Free on itch.io
- **No IAPs**: Never
- **No Ads**: Clean experience
