# Rhythm Rush - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Click**: Earn currency (amount depends on timing)
2. **Purchase Generators**: Spend currency to buy passive income
3. **Upgrade Generators**: Increase generator output
4. **Prestige**: Reset progress for permanent bonuses

## Rules and Constraints
- Clicking on-beat gives 2x currency + combo bonus
- Clicking off-beat gives 0.5x currency + breaks combo
- Generators produce currency automatically
- Each generator type costs more than the last
- Prestige requires reaching specific milestone

## Win Conditions
- No traditional win condition
- Endgame: Reach maximum prestige level
- Goal: Optimize income per second

## Scoring System
- **Base Click Value**: 1 currency
- **On-Beat Bonus**: 2x multiplier
- **Combo Multiplier**: +0.1x per consecutive on-beat click (max 5x)
- **Off-Beat Penalty**: 0.5x multiplier + combo reset

## Difficulty Progression
- Early game: Manual clicking dominant
- Mid game: Balance of clicking and generators
- Late game: Generators dominant, clicking for bonuses
- Prestige: Restart with permanent bonuses

## Generator Types (6 Total)
1. **Beat Maker** - 10 currency, produces 1/sec
2. **Rhythm Engine** - 100 currency, produces 10/sec
3. **Tempo Tower** - 1,000 currency, produces 100/sec
4. **Harmony Hub** - 10,000 currency, produces 1,000/sec
5. **Symphony Station** - 100,000 currency, produces 10,000/sec
6. **Orchestra Nexus** - 1,000,000 currency, produces 100,000/sec

## Upgrade System
- Each generator can be upgraded 10 times
- Each upgrade doubles production
- Upgrade cost increases exponentially
