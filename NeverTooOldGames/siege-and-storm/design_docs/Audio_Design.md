# Siege and Storm - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Epic medieval, orchestral
- **Tempo**: Medium, dramatic
- **Mood**: Strategic, intense
- **Style**: Fantasy battle music

## Sound Effects List

### Tower Actions
- **Tower Place**: Building sound
- **Tower Attack**: Weapon-specific sounds
- **Tower Upgrade**: Power-up sound
- **Tower Sell**: Destruction sound

### Enemy Actions
- **Enemy Spawn**: Portal sound
- **Enemy Movement**: Footsteps
- **Enemy Death**: Death cry
- **Enemy Reach Base**: Alarm

### Game Events
- **Wave Start**: Horn blast
- **Wave Complete**: Victory chime
- **Phase Change**: Dramatic transition
- **Level Complete**: Epic fanfare
- **Level Failed**: Defeat horn

### UI
- **Button Click**: Stone tap
- **Currency Gain**: Coin sound
- **Insufficient Funds**: Negative buzz

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Tower placement sounds instant
- **Spatial**: Sounds positioned on map
- **Layered**: Multiple towers create battle ambience

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
