# Siege and Storm - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions

### Defense Phase
1. **Place Tower**: Click spot, select tower type
2. **Upgrade Tower**: Click tower, pay to upgrade
3. **Sell Tower**: Reclaim partial cost
4. **Start Wave**: Begin next enemy wave

### Attack Phase
1. **Spawn Unit**: Click tower to spawn unit
2. **Manage Resources**: Balance unit spawning
3. **Strategic Timing**: Send units in waves

## Rules and Constraints
- Towers can only be placed on designated spots
- Limited currency for tower placement
- Enemies follow predetermined paths
- Towers auto-attack in range
- Must survive all defense waves to attack

## Win Conditions
- Survive all defense waves
- Destroy enemy base in attack phase

## Lose Conditions
- Enemy reaches your base (defense phase)
- Run out of resources in attack phase

## Scoring System
- **Enemies Killed**: Points per kill
- **Waves Survived**: Bonus per wave
- **Base Health Remaining**: Bonus for no damage
- **Time Bonus**: Faster completion

## Star Rating
- **3 Stars**: Perfect defense + fast attack
- **2 Stars**: Good defense + complete attack
- **1 Star**: Complete level

## Tower Types (5-6)

### Arrow Tower
- Cost: 100
- Damage: Medium
- Range: Medium
- Attack Speed: Fast

### Cannon Tower
- Cost: 200
- Damage: High (splash)
- Range: Long
- Attack Speed: Slow

### Magic Tower
- Cost: 150
- Damage: Medium
- Range: Medium
- Special: Slows enemies

### Barracks
- Cost: 250
- Spawns: Ground units
- Special: Blocks path

### Archer Tower
- Cost: 120
- Damage: Low
- Range: Very Long
- Attack Speed: Very Fast

## Difficulty Progression (10 Levels)
- **Level 1-3**: Simple paths, basic enemies
- **Level 4-6**: Complex paths, varied enemies
- **Level 7-9**: Multiple paths, tough enemies
- **Level 10**: Boss level, all mechanics
