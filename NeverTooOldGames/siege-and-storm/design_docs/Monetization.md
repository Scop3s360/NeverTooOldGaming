# Siege and Storm - Monetization

## Business Model
**Paid ($3.99-4.99)**

## Pricing Strategy
- **itch.io**: Pay what you want (suggested $3.99)
- **Steam**: $4.99 fixed price
- **Mobile**: $2.99 one-time purchase

## What Players Get
- Complete game
- All 10 levels
- Both game phases
- All tower types
- No ads
- No IAPs
- Full features

## Monetization Rules (NEVER)
- ❌ No in-app purchases
- ❌ No ads
- ❌ No premium towers
- ❌ No pay-to-win
- ❌ No subscriptions

## Why Paid?
- Most complex game in portfolio
- Dual-phase gameplay
- High production value
- Fair value for content
- Premium experience

## Success Metrics
- Sales count
- Player retention
- Completion rate
- Positive reviews
- Average playtime
