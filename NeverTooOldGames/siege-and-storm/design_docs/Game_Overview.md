# Siege and Storm - Game Overview

## Title
**Siege and Storm**

## Tagline
"Defend, Then Attack - Master Both Sides of Battle"

## High-Level Concept
Dual-phase tower defense where you defend against enemy waves, then use your towers to spawn units and attack the enemy base. Combines traditional tower defense with reverse tower defense in a single game, offering strategic depth and variety.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Tower defense fans
- Strategy game enthusiasts
- Players who enjoy tactical gameplay

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (touch-friendly strategy)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Level**: 10-15 minutes
- **10 Levels**: 2-3 hours
- **Replayability**: High (different strategies)

## Monetization
- **Model**: Paid ($3.99-4.99) or Free with donation
- **No IAPs**: Never
- **No Ads**: Clean experience
