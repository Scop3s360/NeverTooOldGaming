# Mirror Maze - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Move**: Arrow keys or WASD
2. **Jump**: Space (both characters jump)
3. **Restart**: R to restart level
4. **Undo**: Z to undo last move (optional)

## Rules and Constraints
- Both characters move simultaneously
- Shadow character mirrors or reverses movements
- Both must reach their goals to complete level
- Single-screen puzzles (no scrolling)
- Environmental obstacles affect each differently

## Mirror Modes

### Horizontal Mirror
- Shadow moves opposite horizontally
- Move right → Shadow moves left
- Jump → Both jump

### Vertical Mirror
- Shadow moves opposite vertically
- Move up → Shadow moves down

### Reverse
- Shadow moves in exact opposite direction
- Move right → Shadow moves left
- Move up → Shadow moves down

## Win Conditions
- Both characters reach their respective goals simultaneously

## Lose Conditions
- Either character dies (falls, hits hazard)
- Can retry unlimited times

## Scoring System
- **Move Count**: Fewer moves = better
- **Time**: Faster completion = better
- **Par**: Expected moves for each level

## Difficulty Progression (10 Levels)
- **Level 1-3**: Simple horizontal mirror
- **Level 4-6**: Vertical mirror introduced
- **Level 7-9**: Reverse mode, complex obstacles
- **Level 10**: Master puzzle combining all modes
