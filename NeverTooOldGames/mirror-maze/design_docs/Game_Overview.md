# Mirror Maze - Game Overview

## Title
**Mirror Maze**

## Tagline
"Two Minds, One Goal - Navigate the Dual Reality"

## High-Level Concept
A puzzle platformer where you control two characters simultaneously - one is the shadow/mirror of the other. Both must reach their respective goals to complete each level. One character moves normally while the shadow moves in reverse or mirrored patterns, creating brain-bending spatial puzzles.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Puzzle game enthusiasts
- Platformer fans
- Players who enjoy spatial reasoning challenges

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (touch controls)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Level**: 2-5 minutes
- **10 Levels**: 30-60 minutes
- **Replayability**: Medium (puzzle solutions)
