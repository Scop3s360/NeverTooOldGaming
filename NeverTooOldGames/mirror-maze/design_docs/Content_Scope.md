# Mirror Maze - Content Scope

## Levels (10 Total)

### Level 1: Tutorial - Horizontal Mirror
- Simple layout
- Introduces horizontal mirroring
- Par: 5 moves

### Level 2: Basic Obstacles
- Platforms and gaps
- Horizontal mirror
- Par: 7 moves

### Level 3: Timing Challenge
- Moving platforms
- Horizontal mirror
- Par: 10 moves

### Level 4: Vertical Introduction
- Introduces vertical mirroring
- Simple layout
- Par: 8 moves

### Level 5: Mixed Mirrors
- Switches between mirror modes
- Medium complexity
- Par: 12 moves

### Level 6: Hazards
- Spikes and death zones
- Careful planning required
- Par: 10 moves

### Level 7: Reverse Mode
- Introduces full reverse
- Complex pathing
- Par: 15 moves

### Level 8: Multi-Room
- Multiple connected areas
- All mirror modes
- Par: 18 moves

### Level 9: Precision
- Tight spaces
- Exact movements required
- Par: 20 moves

### Level 10: Master Maze
- All mechanics combined
- Longest puzzle
- Par: 25 moves

## Progression Milestones
- Complete first level
- Master horizontal mirroring
- Master vertical mirroring
- Master reverse mode
- Complete all 10 levels
- Beat par on all levels

## Replayability Features
- Replay for better move count
- Try to beat par
- Speedrun potential
- Different solution paths
