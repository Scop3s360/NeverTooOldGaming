# Mirror Maze - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Ambient, puzzle music
- **Tempo**: Slow, contemplative
- **Mood**: Mysterious, thoughtful
- **Style**: Minimalist, atmospheric

## Sound Effects List

### Character Actions
- **Move**: Soft footstep
- **Jump**: Light whoosh
- **Land**: Soft thud
- **Death**: Negative tone

### Game Events
- **Goal Reached**: Positive chime
- **Level Complete**: Victory jingle
- **Mirror Switch**: Ethereal sound
- **Restart**: Reset sound

### UI
- **Button Click**: Soft tap
- **Undo**: Reverse whoosh
- **Level Select**: Menu beep

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Movement sounds instant
- **Synchronized**: Both characters make sounds
- **Spatial**: Stereo positioning for each character

## Ambient Sounds
- Subtle atmospheric hum
- Echo effects for shadow character

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
