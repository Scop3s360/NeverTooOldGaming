# Letter Ladder - Game Overview

## Title
**Letter Ladder**

## Tagline
"One Letter at a Time - Climb from Word to Word"

## High-Level Concept
A word puzzle game where players transform one word into another by changing one letter at a time. Each intermediate step must be a valid word. Combines Wordle-style word validation with strategic puzzle-solving.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Word game enthusiasts
- Puzzle lovers
- Educational market (vocabulary building)

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (perfect for touch typing)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Puzzle**: 2-5 minutes
- **10 Levels**: 20-50 minutes
- **Daily Challenge**: 5 minutes per day

## Monetization
- **Model**: Free on itch.io
- **No IAPs**: Never
- **No Ads**: Clean experience
