# Letter Ladder - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Ambient, calm, focus music
- **Tempo**: Slow, relaxing
- **Mood**: Thoughtful, contemplative
- **Volume**: Subtle, non-distracting

## Sound Effects List

### Word Interactions
- **Letter Type**: Soft keyboard click
- **Valid Word**: Pleasant chime
- **Invalid Word**: Gentle negative tone
- **Puzzle Complete**: Victory jingle

### UI Interactions
- **Button Click**: Soft tap
- **Hint Used**: Helpful "ding"
- **Undo**: Reverse whoosh
- **Star Earned**: Twinkle sound

### Progression
- **Level Unlock**: Unlock chime
- **3-Star Complete**: Special fanfare

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Typing sounds instant
- **Delayed**: Validation sound after submit
- **Layered**: Multiple sounds for complete

## Ambient Sounds
- Optional subtle background ambience
- Quiet, focus-friendly

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
