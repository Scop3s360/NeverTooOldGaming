# Letter Ladder - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Select Letter**: Click letter to change
2. **Type Letter**: Enter new letter
3. **Submit Word**: Confirm word change
4. **Undo**: Revert last move
5. **Hint**: Get suggested next word (limited)

## Rules and Constraints
- Change exactly one letter per move
- Each step must be a valid English word
- Cannot repeat words in same puzzle
- Limited moves for star ratings

## Win Conditions
- Transform start word into target word
- Use valid words for all steps

## Lose Conditions
- No lose condition (can retry unlimited)
- Fail to achieve star rating (optional challenge)

## Scoring System
- **Par**: Expected number of moves
- **3 Stars**: Complete at or under par
- **2 Stars**: Complete within par + 2
- **1 Star**: Complete puzzle (any moves)

## Difficulty Progression

### 10 Levels
- **Level 1-3**: 3-letter words, 2-3 moves
- **Level 4-6**: 4-letter words, 3-4 moves
- **Level 7-9**: 4-5 letter words, 4-5 moves
- **Level 10**: 5-letter words, 5+ moves

## Hint System
- 3 hints per puzzle
- Shows one valid next word
- Costs star rating if used
