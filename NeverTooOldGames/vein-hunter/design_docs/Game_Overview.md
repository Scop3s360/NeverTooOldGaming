# Vein Hunter - Game Overview

## Title
**Vein Hunter**

## Tagline
"Dig Deep, Strike Rich - Build Your Mining Empire"

## High-Level Concept
A futuristic dwarven mining idle clicker where players dig deeper to find minerals, gems, and rare resources. Click to earn currency, buy generators (miners) that earn automatically, upgrade tools, and use a prestige system for long-term progression. Combines active clicking with idle mechanics.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Idle/clicker game fans
- Mining/resource management enthusiasts
- Players who enjoy progression systems

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (perfect for idle gameplay)

## Estimated Playtime
- **Single Session**: 15-30 minutes active, infinite idle
- **Total Content**: 10+ hours to reach endgame
- **Idle Progression**: Earn while away (up to 24 hours)

## Monetization
- **Model**: Free on itch.io
- **No IAPs**: Never
- **No Ads**: Clean experience
