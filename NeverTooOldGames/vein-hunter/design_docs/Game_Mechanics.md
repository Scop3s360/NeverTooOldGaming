# Vein Hunter - Game Mechanics

## Player Actions
1. **Click to Mine**: Earn currency per click
2. **Purchase Miners**: Buy automated generators
3. **Upgrade Miners**: Increase output
4. **Upgrade Tools**: Increase click power
5. **Prestige**: Reset for permanent bonuses

## Rules and Constraints
- Currency earned through clicking and miners
- Miners produce currency automatically
- Exponential cost scaling
- Prestige requires minimum currency threshold
- Offline progress capped at 24 hours

## Win Conditions
- No traditional win condition (idle game)
- Reach endgame milestones
- Max out all miners
- Achieve prestige goals

## Lose Conditions
- No lose condition

## Scoring System
- **Click Power**: Currency per click
- **Income Per Second**: From all miners
- **Total Earned**: Lifetime currency
- **Prestige Level**: Permanent multiplier

## Miner Types (6-8)

### Pickaxe Dwarf
- Cost: 10
- Income: 0.1/sec
- Entry level

### Drill Operator
- Cost: 100
- Income: 1/sec
- Early game

### Laser Miner
- Cost: 1,000
- Income: 10/sec
- Mid game

### Plasma Excavator
- Cost: 10,000
- Income: 100/sec
- Late mid game

### Quantum Digger
- Cost: 100,000
- Income: 1,000/sec
- Late game

### Singularity Mine
- Cost: 1M
- Income: 10,000/sec
- Endgame

## Prestige System
- Unlock at 1M total currency earned
- Reset all progress
- Gain permanent multiplier (2x, 3x, etc.)
- Multiple prestige levels
- Exponential growth

## Difficulty Progression
- Early: Clicking dominant
- Mid: Balance of clicking and miners
- Late: Miners dominant
- Endgame: Prestige optimization
