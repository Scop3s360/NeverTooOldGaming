# Vein Hunter - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Electronic, industrial
- **Tempo**: Medium, steady
- **Mood**: Productive, motivating
- **Style**: Futuristic mining ambience

## Sound Effects List

### Mining Actions
- **Click**: Satisfying "thunk" or laser sound
- **Rock Break**: Crumbling sound
- **Resource Found**: Special chime per resource type
- **Rare Discovery**: Epic sound

### Miner Actions
- **Purchase**: Unlock/summon sound
- **Upgrade**: Power-up sound
- **Miner Working**: Ambient drilling/mining loops

### Progression
- **Level Up**: Achievement sound
- **Prestige**: Epic fanfare
- **Milestone**: Special notification sound

### UI
- **Button Click**: Metallic tap
- **Tab Switch**: Swoosh
- **Insufficient Funds**: Negative buzz

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Click sounds instant
- **Layered**: Multiple miners create industrial ambience
- **Satisfying**: Clear audio rewards

## Ambient Sounds
- Cave ambience
- Distant machinery
- Mineral resonance
- Futuristic hums

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
