# Heavy Paddle - Game Overview

## Title
**Heavy Paddle**

## Tagline
"Master the Weight - Every Move Matters"

## High-Level Concept
Pong with physics-based paddle momentum. The more you move your paddle, the heavier and slower it becomes. Stay still and it becomes light and fast. This adds strategic depth to classic Pong gameplay.

## Target Audience
- Casual players of all ages
- Arcade game fans
- Pong enthusiasts looking for a twist
- Local multiplayer fans

## Platform
- **Primary**: Windows PC
- **Future**: Mobile (touch controls)

- **Session**: 15-30 minutes
- **Replayability**: High (competitive)
