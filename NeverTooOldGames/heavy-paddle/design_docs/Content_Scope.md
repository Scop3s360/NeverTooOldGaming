# Heavy Paddle - Content Scope

## Game Modes (3)

### 1. Single Player vs AI
- 4 difficulty levels
- Practice against computer
- High score tracking

### 2. Local 2-Player
- Competitive multiplayer
- Same keyboard or dual controllers
- Best of 3/5/7 matches

### 3. Practice Mode
- No scoring
- Experiment with mechanics
- Learn weight management

## AI Difficulty Levels (4)

### Easy
- Slow reaction time
- Poor positioning
- Doesn't manage weight well

### Medium
- Good reaction time
- Decent positioning
- Basic weight management

### Hard
- Fast reaction time
- Strategic positioning
- Good weight management

### Expert
- Perfect reaction time
- Optimal positioning
- Master weight management

## Progression Milestones
- Beat Easy AI
- Beat Medium AI
- Beat Hard AI
- Beat Expert AI
- Win 10 matches
- Win 50 matches

## Replayability Features
- Competitive multiplayer
