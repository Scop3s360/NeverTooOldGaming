# Heavy Paddle - Audio Design

## Music Style and Mood
- **Genre**: Retro arcade, chiptune
- **Tempo**: Medium, steady
- **Mood**: Competitive, focused
- **Style**: 8-bit inspired

## Sound Effects List

### Gameplay
- **Paddle Hit**: Classic Pong "boop" (pitch varies with weight)
- **Wall Bounce**: Higher pitched "boop"
- **Score**: Victory chime
- **Miss**: Negative buzz

### Weight System
- **Weight Increase**: Subtle low tone
- **Weight Decrease**: Subtle high tone
- **Max Weight**: Warning beep

### Game Events
- **Match Start**: Ready beep
- **Match Win**: Victory fanfare
- **Match Lose**: Defeat sound

### UI
- **Button Click**: Retro beep
- **Menu Navigate**: Soft tick

## Audio Feedback for Actions
- **Immediate**: Hit sounds play on contact
- **Pitch Variation**: Heavier paddle = lower pitch hit
- **Spatial**: Stereo positioning for ball location

## Volume Controls
- Master volume
- Music volume
- SFX volume
- Mute toggle
